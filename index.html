About

I am a Staff Research Engineer in Wireless Communications with a PhD from UT Austin. My work spans PHY-layer innovation, mmWave and FR3 systems, and stochastic modeling of wireless networks. I’m a co-inventor on 20+ patents and author of over 25 peer-reviewed publications.
